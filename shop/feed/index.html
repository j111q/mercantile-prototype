]]> Add a touch of charm to your wardrobe or accessories with this classic WordPress iron-on patch! Whether for your laptop bag, backpack, or jacket, the WordPress patch is a simple and stylish way to celebrate your love for WordPress!

 Details:

  • Iron on patch approx 2″.

How to Apply Your Patch:

  1. Preheat your iron to medium-high heat.

  2. Place your garment on a flat, hard surface.

  3. Position the patch where you want it, with the design facing up.

  4. Cover the patch with a thin cloth or fabric.

  5. Press firmly with the heated iron for 45-60 seconds.

  6. Flip the garment inside-out and iron the back of the patch for 30 seconds.

  7. Check adhesion gently. Repeat steps 5-6 if needed.

  8. Let your garment cool before wearing.

Tips:

  • Works best on cotton and denim for semi-permanent adhesion.

  • Not recommended for synthetic fabrics.

  • For a permanent fix, sew or use fabric glue.
